Do you need to re-energize a lesson so you can better engage your students? Consider the ENGAGE method.

Think about what you normally do during your Day One syllabus lessons, semester after semester. After applying the ENGAGE steps, your lesson can become more interactive, meaningful, and student-centered.

The ENGAGE Method

Step 1: Energize
Prepare your students for learning.

Step 2: Navigate Content
Guide students through the content and support high-level learning.

Step 3: Generate Meaning
Help students make meaning from the material and involve them in their own learning process.

Step 4: Apply to the Real World
Connect learning to real-world situations and, when possible, showcase student learning.

Step 5: Gauge and Celebrate
Assess student understanding and celebrate progress. How can you encourage meaningful learning?

Step 6: Extend Learning
Continue the learning process and make it active beyond the initial lesson.
